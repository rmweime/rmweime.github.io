Play 2048

What is 2048?

2048 is a simple but addictive puzzle game where you use arrow keys to merge numbered tiles until you reach the number 2048. It's perfect for quick mental workouts and surprisingly strategic.

How to Play

Use your arrow keys to slide the numbered tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one. Keep combining them to reach the elusive 2048 tile!

  • Arrow keys control movement (Up, Down, Left, Right)
  • Each move adds a new tile to the board
  • The game ends when no moves are left
